About Us

At Magic Scrubs, we deliver top-tier mobile car detailing services directly to your doorstep. Our team takes pride in restoring the shine and freshness of every vehicle — inside and out.

What We Do

We offer a full spectrum of services from exterior hand washes and protective wax applications to deep interior shampooing, leather conditioning, and stain removal. Every detail is meticulously cleaned, polished, and protected.

Our Goal

To exceed expectations with every service, leaving your vehicle looking—and feeling—brand new. We aim to build lasting relationships by delivering convenience, quality, and care.

What Is Car Detailing?

Car detailing is more than a simple car wash. It’s a comprehensive, deep-clean and restoration process that addresses paint imperfections, interior stains, and protective coatings. Detailing preserves your car’s appearance, protects its value, and enhances your driving experience.

Why Get Detailing?

  • Protects paint from environmental damage
  • Removes stubborn stains & odors inside
  • Restores showroom-quality shine
  • Helps maintain resale value
  • Personalized care tailored to your vehicle
